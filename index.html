PlasmaTV Linux

An Arch Linux-based distro that boots straight into a TV-first KDE Plasma Bigscreen experience, with a full Plasma Desktop available one click away.

Quick overview

📺 Plasma Bigscreen

KDE's TV-optimized 10-foot UI. Navigate with a remote or keyboard from the couch.

🖥️ Full desktop

Switch to the classic Plasma Desktop anytime. One click, no reboot.

📦 25 streaming apps

Netflix, YouTube, Spotify, Disney+, Prime Video, and more — each a native-feeling kiosk app.

🔐 Parental controls

Child accounts with PIN locks, screen time limits, DNS filtering, and a restricted shell.

⚡ Instant boot

Plymouth splash, auto-login, straight to the user selector. No delays or login prompts.

🐧 Arch Linux base

Rolling release, AUR access, full pacman ecosystem. All the power of Arch under the TV.
